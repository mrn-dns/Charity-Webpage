Sebastian Vettel
Born in 1987
Sebastian Vettel is a German racing driver who competes in Formula One for Aston Martin, having previously driven for BMW Sauber, Toro Rosso, Red Bull and Ferrari.Vettel is the youngest World Champion in Formula 1, he also has the third most race victories (53) and podium finishes (122) and the fourth most pole positions (57).
